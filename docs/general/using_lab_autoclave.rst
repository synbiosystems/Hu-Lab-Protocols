Using the lab Autoclave
========================
Note: These instructions are taken directly from manual. These instructions are featured on a sticker on top of the autoclave, except the bold portions here. Please note the **bold** portions.

1. Move the main power switch on the back of the device up to the ON position.
2. Move the ON/OFF rocker power switch, located on the front panel, to the ON position. Green power light will illuminate.
3. Turn the red pressure/temperature tracking needle to 0 psi. It will indicate the highest pressure reached during the cucle.
4. Open the front door of the autoclave and set the operation knob to the FILL WATER position.

   4.1. Water flows in immediately (If not, fill water reservoir with DI up to just below the pressure relief valve).
   
   4.2. When water reaches the mark at the front of the autoclave (indentation in the chamber), turn the operation knob immediately to STERILIZE.
   
   **Warning: The knob should be turned in a clockwise direction only!**

5. Load autoclave.
6. Shut door, move clamp into position, and tighten to a firm "hand tight". Do not overtighten as this may damage the gasket.
7. Turn the thermostat knob to the desired sterilization temperature.
8. Turn the timer knob to the desired time. Heat light turns on. Light will cycle on/off when at the desired setpoint as it controls for the temperature.
9. If not liquid contents, turn operation knob to EXHAUST/DRY position **before pressure drops before 10 psi**. This returns steam and leftover water to the reservoir and dropping the pressure gauge to 0 psi.

   9.1. If liquid, wait for pressure to decrease on its own.

10. If drying is not required, turn the operation knob to "0", and remove contents. Turn off device.
11. If drying is required, unscrew the door clamp fully, allowing the door to open half and inch. Leave door there, with heat still activated for 20-30 minutes.
12. Remove contents once drying is complete. Turn operation knob to "0". Turn off device.
